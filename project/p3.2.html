Founded in 1987 by the Hernandez family, Mexicana Fiesta began as a small dream to bring authentic Mexican flavors to our community. What started as a humble family kitchen has grown into a beloved restaurant that serves thousands of satisfied customer every year.

Our recipes have been passed down through four generations, each adding their own special touch while preserving the traditional methods that make our food truly exceptional.
